Expert Instruction

Career Development

Our career development program runs throughout the 6 weeks, preparing students for future professional success by helping them identify their career goals, develop personal branding, and create actionable career plans. About Career Catalyst

6 weeks. Lifetime impact.

Career Catalyst offers two intensive 6-week bootcamps perfect for ambitious, hardworking, STEM-focused high schoolers starting in June 2025.

Our mission: teach high schoolers the skills they need to excel in the professional world & how to network efficiently to get the opportunities they are passionate about.

You could take years making the same mistakes all high schoolers make when jumping headfirst into developing a professional career. Or, you could get guidance and expert opinion from our experienced instructors who have been through it all, and will guide you every step of the way.

If you're a student interested in boosting your career opportunities, our bootcamps are what you need to skyrocket your future. You'll gain practical skills, build real projects, and develop a professional network that will serve you for years to come.

Upon completion, you'll receive a professional certificate that you can proudly display on your LinkedIn profile and include in your resume for college applications and internships, demonstrating your commitment to professional development and the valuable skills you've acquired.
