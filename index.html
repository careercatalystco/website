What We Do For You

5 years of knowledge and experience in 3 days. Here's some topics we cover.

Linkedin Profile Optimization

Our LinkedIn profile optimization service helps students create and refine their LinkedIn profiles to stand out to recruiters and industry professionals. We guide them through best practices for crafting compelling headlines, summaries, and experiences, ensuring their profiles accurately reflect their skills, achievements, and career aspirations.

How to Network Efficiently

Our networking efficiency training teaches students the importance of building and maintaining professional relationships. Through interactive workshops and role-playing exercises, they learn effective networking strategies, including how to approach industry professionals, maintain connections, and leverage networks for career opportunities.

AI and Machine Learning Basics

In our AI and machine learning basics course, students receive an introduction to the fundamental concepts of artificial intelligence and machine learning. Through hands-on exercises and real-world examples, they learn how these technologies are transforming various industries and how to apply basic programming skills to develop simple AI models.

The Ethics of AI

Understanding the ethical implications of AI is crucial for future innovators. Our ethics of AI course explores the moral and societal impacts of AI technology. Students engage in discussions and case studies to learn about responsible AI development, data privacy, and the importance of ethical decision-making in tech.

Career Development

Our career development program prepares students for future professional success by helping them identify their career goals, develop personal branding, and create actionable career plans. Through resume building, interview preparation, and job search strategies, we equip them with the tools needed to navigate their career paths confidently.

Capstone Projects

The Build-Your-Own Capstone Projects segment allows students to apply their newly acquired skills to real-world problems. They work on individual or group projects, from concept to completion, showcasing their abilities in areas such as AI application development, portfolio creation, or innovative product design. These projects serve as a tangible demonstration of their competencies and creativity.

About Career Catalyst

3 days. 4 hours. Lifetime impact.

Career Catalyst is a 3-day intensive in-person bootcamp perfect for ambitious, hardworking, STEM-focused Bay Area highschoolers hosted in mid-August.

Our mission: teach highschoolers the skills they need to excel in the professional world & how to network efficiently to get the opportunities they are passionate about.

You could take a few years making the same mistakes all high schoolers make when jumping headfirst into developing a professional career. Or, you could get guidance and expert opinion from someone who's been through it all, and will guide you every minute of the way. Which leads to a higher chance of success?

If you're a student interested in boosting your door of opportunities, Career Catalyst is what you need to skyrocket your career. You will figure out the learnings of 5+ years, all condensed into 3 days. No fluff, simply straightforward information to help you reach your professional goals.
